About Me

Hi, I'm Rens Pronk. From The Hague, I have a passion for UX design and aspire to become a UX designer. Currently, I work at Studentaanhuis where I help people with their computer problems at home. Through this experience, I've developed a strong ability to work solution-oriented and effectively communicate with customers.

In my free time, I enjoy working out, practicing photography, and watching movies. These hobbies allow me to unwind and stay creative, which I believe helps me in my design work.

I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to develop my skills in UX design. I am excited to connect with others in the industry and collaborate on innovative projects that can make a positive impact on people's lives.

Skills & Experience

As a UX designer, I possess extensive experience and expertise in various aspects of the field. My skill set encompasses conducting thorough research, crafting intuitive designs, developing prototypes, and conducting comprehensive user testing. With a holistic approach to UX design, I strive to create user-centered solutions that meet the needs and expectations of the target audience.
