Correct

  • Client: Correct.nl
  • Team: Indevidual
  • Year: 2020
  • Deliverables: Prototype

For my assignment for Correct.nl I had the opportunity to delve into the world of design patterns. This assignment focused on revamping the webshop's homepage and creating product and checkout pages while incorporating an extensive range of design patterns. It proved to be a captivating and enlightening experience that significantly contributed to my growth as a designer.

My main objective was to develop a captivating homepage that would enhance the browsing experience for visitors. By leveraging design patterns such as card-based layouts, navigation menus, and prominent call-to-action buttons, I aimed to create an intuitive and visually engaging interface. As the project progressed, I delved into designing product and checkout pages, where I utilized design patterns like breadcrumbs, product image galleries, user reviews, and a streamlined checkout process. These patterns optimized the user journey, ensuring a smooth path from product selection to final purchase.

In the end, I delivered a fully functional prototype created with Figma, encompassing the redesigned homepage, detailed product pages, and a streamlined checkout process. This assignment not only showcased my ability to integrate various design patterns but also inspired me to explore and utilize them further in future design projects. The experience reinforced the importance of consistency, familiarity, and user-centered design, fueling my commitment to crafting exceptional digital experiences that resonate with users and drive business success.

Process

During the course of this assignment, our primary focus was to leverage design patterns effectively. Unlike previous projects, all the pages were predetermined, eliminating the need for extensive research or user interviews. Our journey began in the classroom, where we studied and gathered various design patterns to be employed in this endeavor.

The initial phase involved creating wireframes for each page and carefully selecting the appropriate patterns to enhance usability and user experience. Utilizing Figma as my design tool of choice, I proceeded to construct the pages and populate them with content extracted from the existing website. To optimize efficiency, we adhered to strict guidelines regarding the selection of products, ensuring that valuable time was not spent on unnecessary details. From navigation patterns to layout choices, I integrated an array of patterns that aligned with the project's objectives.

As the patterns fell into place, I transitioned towards refining the website's overall style, injecting a fresh and contemporary look into the design. With a clear vision in mind, I began meticulously crafting the final prototype, bringing together the intricate patterns, engaging content, and compelling visuals to provide an immersive user experience.

Through this process, I gained a deeper understanding of the importance of design patterns in creating cohesive and user-friendly interfaces. The project enabled me to hone my skills in pattern selection and application, ultimately empowering me to design more efficient and intuitive digital experiences in the future.

Results

After putting in dedicated effort, I successfully completed the full prototype of the website using Figma, transforming it into an interactive experience. To highlight the effective utilization of design patterns throughout the project, I created separate images that specifically pointed out the patterns implemented in various sections of the website.

The color scheme of the website remained consistent with the original design, but I introduced a more cohesive and visually pleasing style. By incorporating design patterns strategically, the usability of the website was significantly enhanced. Users were able to navigate through the pages effortlessly, benefiting from the familiarity and intuitive nature of the implemented patterns.

The comprehensive application of design patterns not only improved the overall user experience but also contributed to a more streamlined and harmonious design. The website exuded a professional and polished aesthetic, effectively communicating the brand identity of Correct.nl.

This project served as a testament to the importance of design patterns in web design and their direct impact on usability. Through meticulous attention to detail and a keen understanding of pattern selection, I successfully delivered a prototype that showcased the power of effective design patterns in creating a user-friendly and visually appealing web interface.
